Montero is an adventure activity meta-search engine that discovers and compares adventure activities in India & Nepal.

Montero compares activities at two levels.

  • 1. Compare the prices offered by different tour operators on an activity
  • 2. Compare two different activities side by side

Montero is built to provide all necessary information about an adventure activity that will help you in planning & booking your adventure trip with much ease. We strive to provide the most recent & trusted reviews about an activity from our partner organizations. Besides, we have added a store section where you can shop the essential gear required for your next adventure trip.

The name Montero is appended from Mont, which means Mountain in French. For us, mountains mean adventure. And so, we created our logo & crafted our brand around this idea.
